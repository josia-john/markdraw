# Deforestation
## Causes
* Agriculture causes deforestation for logging / farming

## Effects
* habitats for 80% of species
* Climate Change

## Shapes
* Go get land fast -&gt; strange forms, not strait lines
* Big companies take rectangular patches
* Spread from main line (Highway, River)

## Causes of Deforestation:
### Farming
* Traditional: Slash and burn (Burn forest for farming, sometimes just temporary)
* Subsistence farming (Government providing land to poor people): Settlement of poor people
* Commercial: Commercial cattle ranching (Deliver meat to fast food chains) &amp; soy plantatatio
### Transport
* 12000 km of new roads: Trans-Amazonian highway
* long railways: carajas railway
### Resources
* Wood, Timber: hardwoods like: mahogany, ebony, rosewood
* Minerals: carajas mine
* Hydro-electicity: itaipu dam
### Settlement
Amazonia grows fast (30 million people in 40 years) and people need space. -&gt; burn down forest

## Rates and effects of Deforestation
### Rates
160'000 km^2 / year deforestation (global): 15-40% in 60 years of amazonian rainforest or 15 ha / minute
### Effects
* partial extinction
* Destroying cultures -&gt; lost tribes
* Poor air quality / Pollution
* infertile soil -&gt; abandonement of farms
* climate change (global warming)
