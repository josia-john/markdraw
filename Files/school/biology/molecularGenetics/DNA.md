# Discovering of DNA
## Genetical exchange:
Slimy bacteria (mouse can't attack)
rough bacteria (mouse can attack)

When killing slimy virus and putting it together with the rough bacteria, the rough bacteria becomes slimy.

## DNA, RNA or protein
**Killing all DNA in killed slimy bacteria ➔ no new slimy bacteria**
Killing all RNA in killed slimy bacteria ➔ new slimy bacteria
Killing all proteins in killed slimy bacteria ➔ new slimy bacteria

## Base Pairing
DNA:
adenine (A) thymine (T) guanine (G) and cytosine (C) 
A-T can form bonds (2 H-bonds)
C-G can form bonds (3 H-bonds)

RNA:
adenine (A) uracil (U) guanine (G) and cytosine (C) 
T gets replaced by U. RNA is single stranded.

the bases are connected to each other by H-bonds.

The bases are connected to sugars (ribose for RNA, deoxyribose for DNA). These sugars are then connected to each other over a phosphate.
		
	
	
				
	
	
		One sugar, base and phosphate build a nucleotide. The whole DNA is considered a nucleic acid.

The primes can be counted clockwise by starting with 0 at the O. One can only read from 3' to 5' and build from 5' to 3'.
